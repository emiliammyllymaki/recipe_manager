# Recipe Manager

A responsive Flutter web application for managing your personal recipe collection.

## Description

Recipe Manager lets you store, browse, and organise all your favourite recipes in one place. You can add recipes with ingredients, step-by-step instructions, difficulty level, and category tags. Mark recipes as favourites, search by title or ingredient, and view statistics about your collection.

## Deployed URL

> **https://emiliammyllymaki.github.io/recipe_manager/**

## How to Use

### Adding a Recipe
1. Go to the **Recipes** tab (home screen)
2. Tap **Add Recipe** in the bottom-right corner
3. Fill in the title, category, difficulty, prep time, servings, ingredients and instructions
4. Tap **Create Recipe** to save — data is stored automatically

### Browsing Recipes
- Use the **search bar** to search by title, ingredient, or tag
- Use the **category chips** to filter by Savoury, Sweet, Snack, or Drink
- Tap the **heart icon** in the top bar to show only favourites

### Viewing a Recipe
- Tap any recipe card to open its detail view
- The detail screen shows all info: prep time, servings, difficulty, ingredients, and instructions

### Editing a Recipe
- Open the recipe detail screen and tap the **edit icon** (pencil) in the app bar
- Make your changes and tap **Save Changes**

### Deleting a Recipe
- From the recipe detail screen, tap the **delete icon** and confirm
- Or tap the delete button on a recipe card directly from the home screen

### Favourites
- Tap the heart icon on any recipe card or detail screen to toggle favourite status
- Use the heart icon in the home screen app bar to filter and show only favourites

### Statistics
- Navigate to the **Statistics** tab to see:
  - Total recipes, favourites count, average/quickest/longest prep time
  - Recipes by category (bar chart)
  - Recipes by difficulty (Easy / Medium / Hard)
  - Preparation time distribution
  - Top 5 recipes by number of ingredients

### Settings
- Navigate to the **Settings** tab to:
  - Toggle **Dark Mode**
  - See a summary of your data (total recipes and favourites)
  - **Clear all recipes** if needed
